Privacy Policy

Bitcoin Odyssey respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

We do not collect personal data directly. However, our app uses third-party services, including Google AdMob, which may collect data to provide better advertising experiences.

AdMob Data Collection

AdMob, operated by Google, may collect:

  • Device Information: IP address, device identifiers, operating system version.
  • App Usage Data: Interactions with ads (e.g., clicks, views).
  • Demographic Data: Information like age, gender, and interests may be used for personalized ads.

For more details on how AdMob handles data, please review Google’s Privacy Policy.

How We Use Information

The information collected by AdMob is used to:

  • Serve ads within our app.
  • Personalize ads (if applicable).

We do not directly collect, store, or share any personal information from our users.

Third-Party Services

Our app uses AdMob by Google. AdMob’s practices are governed by Google’s Privacy Policy and the Google AdMob Policy.

User Rights

You can manage your ad preferences or opt out of personalized ads by:

  • Adjusting your device settings (e.g., reset Advertising ID on Android or Limit Ad Tracking on iOS).
  • Visiting Google’s Ad Settings.

Data Security

We do not collect or store user data directly. Any data collected by AdMob is managed securely by Google.
